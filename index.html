What Cal proves

Clarity before dispatch.

Cal is the field-service version of the AtlasFlow pattern: messy input becomes AI interpretation, structured data, human review, and a clear operational next action.

01

Who is this for?

Owners, dispatchers, office managers, and small service teams who need to triage inbound work without missing urgent risk.

02

Product Thesis

Service requests arrive fragmented and inconsistent, forcing dispatchers to interpret urgency, risk, and next steps manually. Cal can turn that unstructured input into clear, prioritized, dispatch-ready work while directing owner attention to the requests that matter most.

03

Validation Question

Does giving dispatchers an AI-generated category, priority, confidence signal, summary, and recommended next action help them triage requests faster and more consistently—while helping owners identify urgent or high-risk jobs sooner?

Build architecture

Simple stack. Clear operating signal.

The final Cal build keeps the stack intentionally lean. Airtable captures intake, stores data, runs the AI step, writes the structured output, and provides the operator views.

Business value

Speed and clarity for small service teams.

Cal reduces the mental triage load on dispatchers and gives owners a cleaner read on what deserves attention. It is not trying to be a full dispatch platform; it proves the workflow that turns operational noise into clear decisions.
